# ParlAI - AI-Powered Parlay Builder App

**Features**

- Dark, sleek homepage matching your design — bold "AI BUILDS YOUR PERFECT PARLAY" headline with gradient accent text
- "Build My Parlay" flow where you can input tonight's games and get AI-generated parlay suggestions with confidence scores
- "Analyze a Pick" flow where you can enter a specific pick and get AI analysis on it
- Sample parlay card on the homepage showing tonight's slate with legs, confidence percentages, and combined odds
- Payment/subscription tiers section with different plan levels (Free, Pro, VIP)
- Smooth animations and micro-interactions on buttons and cards 

**Design**

- Deep dark navy/charcoal background (#0D0F1A style) — no white, no light mode
- Bold uppercase headings with a purple-to-green gradient on accent text ("PERFECT PARLAY")
- Cyan/teal gradient accent line separating sections
- Parlay cards with dark semi-transparent backgrounds, numbered legs with colored circles, and green confidence percentages
- Two main action buttons: filled gradient (Build) and outlined (Analyze)
- Clean monospace-style type for odds and stats, modern sans-serif for headings
- Mobile-native feel with full-width cards and generous spacing

**Screens**

- **Home** — Hero section with tagline, two CTA buttons, and a sample parlay card preview (matches your screenshot)
- **Build Parlay** — Input tonight's games or let AI auto-detect the slate, then see AI-generated parlay with reasoning
- **Analyze Pick** — Enter a specific pick/bet and get AI confidence analysis and breakdown
- **Plans** — Subscription tiers (Free, Pro, VIP) with feature comparisons and pricing
